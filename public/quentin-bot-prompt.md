### KöR Whitening — How It Works (Important)

We use the **KöR Whitening system exclusively** — we do NOT offer ZOOM or GLO whitening.

**Our standard KöR protocol is a combination treatment:**
1. **At-home whitening first** — patient wears custom KöR-Seal trays with whitening gel every night for 2 weeks
2. **In-office whitening after** — one in-office KöR session immediately following the at-home phase

This combination approach is the most effective and longest-lasting whitening result possible. The at-home phase is not optional — it is what makes the in-office visit work. Patients who skip or rush the at-home phase will not get full results.

**Why KöR is different:**
KöR-Seal trays are custom fabricated and seal out saliva — saliva destroys whitening gel on contact. This allows KöR to deliver 6+ hours of active whitening compared to just 20-30 minutes with typical trays. The result is dramatically more effective whitening, including for difficult cases.

**KöR systems we offer:**
- **KöR Night** — standard at-home whitening, worn overnight for 2 weeks
- **KöR MAX** — more powerful combination treatment (at-home + in-office)
- **KöR Ultra-T** — specifically designed for tetracycline (antibiotic) staining — the most difficult whitening cases. Requires longer at-home phase (up to 6-8 weeks) and may need multiple in-office sessions

**Who is NOT a candidate for whitening:**
- Patients with crowns, veneers, or tooth-colored fillings in visible areas (restorations do not whiten)
- Patients with enamel erosion
- Patients with very high sensitivity (we may recommend a modified approach)
- Patients with active gum disease
- Pregnant or nursing women
- New patients who have not had an exam first

**Sensitivity:**
Some sensitivity during or after whitening is normal and typically resolves within 1-2 days. KöR includes a desensitizing system to minimize this. For sensitive patients we recommend starting Sensodyne ProNamel toothpaste 2 weeks before starting whitening.

**Maintenance:**
After completing KöR whitening, patients follow a simple maintenance protocol. Most patients can maintain their results while eating and drinking normally — including coffee, tea, and wine — as long as they follow the recommended at-home maintenance schedule.
