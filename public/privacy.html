Privacy Policy

Quentin Smile Dental (Family, Cosmetic & Implant Dentistry of Brooklyn) — Messaging Assistant. Last updated: July 2026.

This policy explains how Quentin Smile Dental ("we," "us," "our office") handles information when you message our practice through Facebook Messenger or Instagram Direct Messages, including replies handled by our automated messaging assistant.

What we collect

  • Messages you send us through Facebook or Instagram, so we can understand and respond to your question.
  • Your name and profile information as provided to us by Meta when you start a conversation.
  • A phone number, only if you choose to share one so our team can call or text you back.

We do not ask for or collect Social Security numbers, insurance ID numbers, dates of birth, payment details, or detailed medical history through Messenger or Instagram DMs.

How we use it

  • To answer your questions about our services, hours, location, and insurance.
  • To help you schedule an appointment or have a team member reach out to you.
  • To generate helpful, on-topic replies through an automated assistant, with a real team member available to step in at any time.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use your messages for advertising targeting.

Who processes your information

To operate the messaging assistant we use a small number of service providers, strictly to deliver the service above:

  • Meta Platforms — delivers the Facebook and Instagram messages.
  • Anthropic — generates the assistant's replies. Messages are processed to produce a response and are not used to train models.
  • Upstash — briefly stores recent conversation context so the assistant remembers your thread.
  • Resend — emails a callback request to our office when you leave a phone number.
  • Vercel — hosts the software that runs the assistant.

How long we keep it

  • Conversation context is automatically deleted after about 24 hours.
  • If you leave a phone number, our office keeps that callback request as part of our normal patient records so we can follow up.

Your choices

You can stop the conversation at any time, and you can ask us to access or delete information you shared with us over Messenger or Instagram. Contact us using the details below.
